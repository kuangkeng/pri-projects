Hate crimes against people with disabilities were only collected since 1997. Multiple-bias incidents which are motivated by two or more biases are not shown as the figures are too small. The race category refers to White, Black, American Indian/Alaska Native, Asian/Pacific Islander and multiple races. The ethnicity category refers to Hispanic/Latino or other ethnicity/national origin.
