The study covers 192 coastal countries. Mismanaged plastic waste consists of plastic material that is either littered or inadequately disposed of by populations living within 50km of a coast. This waste is not fully contained, so could eventually enter the ocean via inland waterways, wastewater outflows and transport by wind or tides. See methodology for details.
Source: Plastic waste inputs from land into the ocean, Science.
